The Gold Standard
Vermicompost
वर्मीकम्पोस्ट — मिट्टी का सोना
Vermicompost is organic matter digested by Eisenia fetida earthworms — producing a dark, crumbly fertiliser with 5x more nutrients than regular compost. Rich in NPK, humic acid, plant growth hormones, and billions of beneficial microbes. Dr. Organic produces 1500 tonnes/year from 2800 beds — ICAR-tested, zero adulteration. Boosts crop yield 30–50%.

Jeevamrit & Beejamrit

Ancient Wisdom,
Modern Science

जीवामृत और बीजामृत — वैदिक कृषि विज्ञान

Jeevamrit is a fermented biostimulant from cow dung, urine, gram flour, jaggery and soil — activating trillions of soil microbes overnight. Beejamrit is an organic seed treatment that protects seeds from soil-borne diseases and improves germination rate up to 30%. Dr. Sharvan prepares these fresh on the farm using time-tested and science-validated methods.

Dr. Sharvan Kumar Yadav holds a Ph.D. in Agronomy (Organic Farming) from MPUAT, Udaipur. He worked as Senior Research Fellow at ICAR for 15+ years. During the 2020 lockdown, he left research to start with just 17 vermicompost beds at Sundarpura, Jaipur.

His father's cancer from chemical farming became his mission. Today his 5-hectare farm runs 2800 beds, produces 1500 tonnes/year, and has trained 20,000+ farmers — all free.

"My goal was to give good organic manure to farmers across the country, so that more people join organic farming."
